What We Favor @camunda

Open Source

What we build is open source. We are using many other open source frameworks to build our product. It is in our belief and interest to contribute back whenever possible. Check out some examples:

camunda BPMN 2.0 Javascript libraries

Seamless integration of AngularJS into RequireJS

Toolkit for data heavy AngularJS applications

Advanced linking features for DocPad

Innovation

We believe in the necessity for ongoing innovation. Most of the innovation at camunda comes from our own people. Slacktime and Ship-It days support innovation at camunda. Our community is another great source of inspiration.

PHP SDK for camunda BPM

camunda share: Discuss your BPMN 2.0 process model in the cloud

Quality

We are building high quality software. That’s why we favor clean code: unit tested, peer reviewed, acceptance tested and documented. Our jenkins (QA and build infrastructure) is crucial to our success.

Customer Focus

We believe that our users know what they need most. As with any other great product, many people have lots of ideas as to what should be done next. Some are great, some are inspiring, some are...

Fun

Sometimes, new features, green jenkins and fixed bugs are not enough ;) Then we take a beer from our fridge and enjoy it on our comfy couch. We enjoy table soccer and Wii too. Maybe you will even see us laugh…
